Concept: CHANGE-EVENT

DEFINITION	VALUE	An event that represents a change in state, quality, etc., and that can be described in terms of preconditions and after-effects. CHANGE-EVENT is a placeholder that signals that the meaning of the event is expressed in terms of its preconditions and effects. Nothing should be mapped to CHANGE-EVENT that is not described in terms of preconditions and/or effects.
